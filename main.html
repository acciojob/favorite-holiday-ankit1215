Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto, once the capital of Japan, is a beautiful city known for its historic temples, traditional wooden houses, and rich cultural heritage. It's the perfect blend of old-world charm and natural beauty.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine - Famous for its thousands of vibrant red torii gates.
  2. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) - A stunning Zen temple covered in gold leaf.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove - A serene path through towering bamboo stalks.
  4. Gion District - Known for its traditional tea houses and geisha culture.
  5. Kiyomizu-dera Temple - Offers panoramic views of Kyoto from its wooden stage.
